Who We're Talking To

Three audiences. One decision.

A seat at Sakura Quest is never one person's decision. Every month, the content speaks to all three — the student's curiosity, the parent's caution, and the counsellor's recommendation.

Primary

The Student

Class 10–12 · Age 15–18
Culturally drawn to Japan via anime, manga, and gaming — but stuck between "I love Japanese culture" and "I could build a future there."
"Japan feels exciting, but is it a risk? What if I can't find a job? Is this realistic for someone like me?"
What Unlocks Them
Peer proof — students exactly like them who made it work. Instagram Reels & YouTube Shorts.
Decision Maker

The Parent

Primary Veto Holder
The financial and logical decision-maker. Unfamiliar with modern Japan, benchmarked on US/UK/Canada education paths.
"Is Japan safe? Can my child get a job after? How does the cost compare to traditional study-abroad countries?"
What Unlocks Them
Hard comparisons, cost breakdowns, safety data, testimonials. WhatsApp carousels and email explainers.
High Leverage

The Counsellor

School Coach / Career Advisor
Highly influential but underserved. Recommends US/UK/Canada because resources are ready — Japan feels like extra work.
"I don't know enough about Japanese university admissions to confidently recommend it to my students."
What Unlocks Them
Professional resource kits, university factsheets, alumni contacts. LinkedIn and shareable PDFs.

How the 20 Posts Are Built

Four content buckets.

Every social post belongs to one of four buckets. The split shifts month to month — in July, the Kolkata bucket is heaviest. The other three run continuously as evergreen lead magnets for Education Japan.

Roadshow / Kolkata

7 posts
Event-tied, time-sensitive. Drives registrations and footfall for the 12 July Kolkata leg, then converts it into proof for the next city.
  • Kolkata announcement + venue reveal
  • "What you'll walk away with" carousel
  • Participating universities preview
  • Scholarship session teaser
  • Final 48-hour countdown + seat scarcity
  • Event-day live recap reel
  • Post-event highlights + "next stop" tease

Aspiration / Japan Life

5 posts
Evergreen lead magnet for students. Builds the dream and grows reach independent of any event — the top of the funnel.
  • "A day in the life of an Indian student in Tokyo"
  • Cost of living in Japan — student budget breakdown
  • 5 career paths you didn't know Japan leads in
  • Anime-affinity meme post (brand-safe, comments-led)
  • Campus tour reel — a featured university

Proof / Stories

4 posts
Peer and alumni proof. The single highest-converting content type for undecided students and anxious parents alike.
  • Alumni journey — "From [city] to Osaka"
  • Student testimonial reel from SQ 2025
  • Parent testimonial — addressing the fear
  • "Where are they now" — placement success story

Authority / Education

4 posts
Lead magnets for parents and counsellors. Builds Education Japan's credibility as the definitive Japan-education authority in India.
  • JASSO & MEXT scholarships explained
  • Is Japan safe? The data parents ask for
  • Japan vs Canada — honest cost comparison
  • Counsellor resource: admissions timeline factsheet

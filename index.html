Three Audiences. One Decision.

A seat at Sakura Quest is never one person's decision. We create targeted content streams to address the distinct fears and desires of each key stakeholder group.

Primary

The Student

Class 10–12 • Age 15–18

Culturally drawn to Japan via anime, manga, and gaming, but struggles to bridge the gap between "I love Japanese culture" and "I can build a future there."

"Japan feels exciting, but is it a risk? What if I can't find a job? Is this a realistic option for someone like me?"
What Unlocks Them

Peer proof and seeing students exactly like them who made it work. Dynamic Instagram Reels & YouTube Shorts.

Decision Maker

The Parent

Primary Veto Holder

The actual financial and logical decision-maker. Unfamiliar with modern Japanese tech/lifestyle trends and benchmarked on US/UK/Canada education paths.

"Is Japan safe? Can my child get a job post-graduation? How does the cost compare to traditional study-abroad countries?"
What Unlocks Them

Hard comparisons, cost breakdowns, placement safety rates, and testimonials. WhatsApp-friendly carousels and YouTube explainer videos.

High Leverage

The Counsellor

School Coach / Career Advisor

Highly influential but underserved. Frequently recommends US/UK/Canada because resources are readily available, while Japan feels like extra work.

"I don't know enough about Japanese university admissions to confidently recommend it to my students."
What Unlocks Them

Professional resource kits, university factsheets, and alumni contacts. LinkedIn updates and shareable PDFs.
